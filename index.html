About Piggy

Piggy is a thrilling survival horror game on Roblox, where players must work together to solve puzzles and escape a creepy house while avoiding the deadly Piggy. Inspired by the famous *Granny* and *Peppa Pig* characters, Piggy offers an exciting, mysterious experience for all ages.

Game Features

Intense Horror Gameplay

Survive Piggy's relentless pursuit by solving puzzles and unlocking doors. Will you escape or fall victim?

Multiple Maps

Explore varied maps from spooky houses to shopping malls, each with its own unique puzzles and challenges.

Customizable Characters

Express yourself with exclusive skins, accessories, and emotes to make your character stand out in the game.

Co-Op Multiplayer

Team up with your friends or other players to survive together and escape the clutches of Piggy!
